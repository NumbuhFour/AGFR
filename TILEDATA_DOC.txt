All:
	style (string): attached style key

Door:
	level (string): level file to load
	spawnx (int): spawn loc in next map x
	spawny (int): spawn loc in next map y

Sign:
	speaker (string): speaker of the message
	message (string): the message on the sign
	
Light:
	intensity (float 0-1): Intensity of light
	range (float 0-1): Range of light
	r (float 0-1): Color.red of light
	g (float 0-1): Color.green of light
	b (float 0-1): Color.blue of light
